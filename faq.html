FAQ Page

Why use this Bot?

Our discord bot combines the many features that are highly sought during a hackathon. Computer has begun dominating our world, and the rate of hackathons will only incruse. There are many hackthons that leave their organizers and participants in tears due to several issues. We provide free service to make your competition and life much smoother. Our bot does have features that are already in sone discord bots, but what makes out bot unique is that we combine the features together to ensure smooth and happy coding!

How was InterHackBot made?

InterHackBot was made through the combined effort of our team to go day and night to acomplish all of our ambitious goals. We made it though python and json as we completely fell in love with the end-result.

What was the inspiration for InterHackBot?

Our team observes that online hackathon can be complicated sometimes due to various reasons, including organization of information, time zone differences, etc. As a result, we created the InterHackBot
with multiple features aiming to solve all the problems we see.
